About Us

Professional teleprompting with local expertise and national reach.

Our Story

New York Teleprompter is part of the National Teleprompter network, bringing over 25 years of professional teleprompting experience to the Empire State. We've grown from a single-operator service to a trusted partner for broadcast networks, Fortune 500 companies, and production studios across the region.

Our foundation was built on a simple principle: every speaker deserves to deliver their message with confidence. Whether it's a CEO addressing shareholders, a news anchor on live television, or a performer captivating an audience, our job is to make them look and sound their absolute best.

What Sets Us Apart

In the media capital of the world, there's no shortage of teleprompter services. What makes us different is our commitment to the craft. Our operators aren't just equipment technicians—they're performers who understand timing, pace, and the psychology of public speaking.

  • Experience: Thousands of shows across broadcast, corporate, and live event environments.
  • Reliability: 100% on-time delivery and show completion rate.
  • Flexibility: We adapt to your talent's pace, not the other way around.
  • Current Technology: Latest-generation equipment maintained to broadcast standards.
  • Network Support: Seamless coordination for multi-city productions through National Teleprompter.

Part of National Teleprompter

As a member of the National Teleprompter network, we offer seamless coordination for productions spanning multiple states. One point of contact, consistent quality, and unified logistics—wherever your production takes you.

Our Approach

We believe in being partners, not vendors. Before every show, we work with producers and talent to understand the goals, review scripts, and anticipate challenges. During the event, our operators stay fully engaged—adjusting speed, accommodating ad-libs, and ensuring talent always feels supported.

After thousands of productions, we've learned that the best teleprompting is invisible. When we've done our job right, the audience sees only a confident, polished presenter—never the technology behind it.

Markets We Serve

From Manhattan's broadcast studios and corporate headquarters to venues throughout Upstate New York, we provide professional teleprompting services wherever you need us:

  • Long Island and Westchester
  • Albany and the Capital Region
  • Buffalo and Western New York

  • Syracuse and Central New York
  • Throughout the Northeast Region
